Over the past week, I've created Trencher, a 2D roguelike, for the Big Mode 2025 game jam. The game jam theme was "Power", so the game focuses on building underground powerlines between houses after an earthquake damaged your city's infrastructure.

As you dig, you use your wits and some helpful buildings to keep away monsters that try to destroy what you've built. After completing each level, you can upgrade your equipment with your progress saving in between sessions.

Trencher features a fully destructible world, randomly generated terrain and ruins, and a full mining and crafting system.
