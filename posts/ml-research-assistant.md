I've started working as a research assistant in Dr. Abdullah-Al-Zubaer Imran's machine learning and image processing lab at the University of Kentucky.

In this group, I will focus on data-efficient training methods for medical image segmentation models, like multitasking and contrastive learning. This is a very important topic, as training data for medical imaging models can be expensive to produce, even though the utmost accuracy is needed in the medical field.
