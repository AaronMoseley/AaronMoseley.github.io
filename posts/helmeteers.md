Over the past few days, I worked with my friend, Thomas Noll, to create the game Helmeteers for the Winter Melon Game Jam 2024. After judging, Helmeteers placed in the top 10 games of the jam overall!

Helmeteers is a top-down real-time strategy game built with Unity and C#.

In the game, you attempt to take control of a planet on behalf of your employer who desires the resource Neverfindium that is located there. It features a building-placement system, individual unit pathfinding using the A* algorithm, and an intelligent enemy AI that will attempt to prevent your conquest.
