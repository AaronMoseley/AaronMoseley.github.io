I've started working as an undergraduate research assistant in Dr. Christopher Crawford's nuclear physics lab at the University of Kentucky.

In this group, I will work on creating a high-speed data acquisition system for the Neutron Optics Parity and Time-Reversal Experiment (NOPTREX). This data acquisition system will include a firmware for a CAEN digitizer and a readout software to acquire data from the digitizer and create output files.
