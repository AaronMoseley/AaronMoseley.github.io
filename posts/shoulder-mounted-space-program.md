I've officially released my first commerical game, Shoulder-Mounted Space Program! This game features a custom realistic gravity simulation and was developed in Unity with C#.

In Shoulder-Mounted Space Program, you launch yourself from planet to planet, using a rocket launcher and gravity as your only forms of movement. You do your best to explore hostile regions of space while robotic enemies try to stop you.

With two unique game modes, 15 levels where each introduces a new mechanic/enemy, and a final boss fight, Shoulder-Mounted Space Program provides a large amount of variety given its simple concept.

To get this game ready for release, I coordinated a small group of playtesters, artists, and a composer while being the sole developer.
