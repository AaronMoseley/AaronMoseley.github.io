I've developed PolyCL as part of my work in Dr. Imran's lab at the University of Kentucky. It is a novel contrastive learning framework using an example selection strategy leveraging whether slices contain the target organ and to which CT scan each slice belongs. This framework has shown to improve over supervised baseline models and the popular contrastive learning strategy SimCLR. 

This project was published as a 4-page paper to ISBI 2024 and presented as a poster at the 2023 Commonwealth Computational Summit.
