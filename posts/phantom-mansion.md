Phantom Mansion is a hackathon project developed in C# by myself and my friend, Thomas Noll. This game won first place at the Cat Hacks VIII Hackathon at the University of Kentucky. It was also presented to potential future students at the University of Kentucky's E-Day event in 2023.

This is a roguelike game where you explore a haunted house with the goal of finding treasure at the top of a ghost-infested house. Multiple difficulty levels are available and high scores are saved for multiple players. All levels are randomly generated using randomized depth-first search and enemy AI is implemented using breadth-first-search.
